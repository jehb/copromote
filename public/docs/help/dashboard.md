# Dashboard

Welcome to the Promoty Dashboard! This is your central hub for monitoring your marketing activities.

## Stats Overview
At the top of the page, you'll see a quick overview of your current status:
- **Active Projects**: The number of campaigns currently in progress.
- **Total Assets**: The total count of files and assets across all your projects.
- **Upcoming Events**: A count of events scheduled for the current month.

## Quick Links
Use the **Quick Links** section to access your most frequently used tools and external resources. You can manage these links in the Admin settings.

## Recent Projects
The **Recent Projects** list shows the 5 most recently active projects. Click "View" to jump directly to a project's details.

## Navigation
Use the sidebar on the left to navigate to specific sections like Projects, Products, Tasks, and more.
