# Gallery & Assets

Centralized storage for all your creative assets.

## Organization
- **Folders**: Organize images and videos into folders for easy access.
- **Tags**: Use tags to quickly find related assets across different folders.

## Uploading
Drag and drop files to upload them to the gallery. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, GIF, and MP4.

## Usage
Assets stored here can be easily attached to tasks, projects, and social media posts.
