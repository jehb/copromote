# Products Management

The Products section allows you to manage your product catalog.

## Viewing Products
The main view lists all your available products. You can search and filter this list to find specific items.

## Adding a Product
Click the **New Product** button to add a product to your catalog. You'll need to provide:
- **Name**: The name of the product.
- **Description**: A brief description.
- **Price**: The standard price (if applicable).
- **SKU**: Stock Keeping Unit identifier.

## Editing Products
Click on any product in the list to view its details and make edits. You can update information, change images, and manage inventory settings.
