# Social Media Help

## Overview
Manage your social media content pipeline. Create, schedule, and publish posts across multiple platforms with AI-powered suggestions.

## Creating a Post

1. Click **Create Post** in the top-right corner
2. Fill in post details:
   - **Content**: Your post text (required)
   - **Platform**: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, or Twitter
   - **Scheduled Date**: When to publish (optional)
   - **Status**: Draft, Ready for Review, Scheduled, or Published. 
3. **Assets & Syncing**: You can attach media assets to your posts directly from the gallery. Once your post is marked as "Ready for Review" or "Published", it is automatically synchronized with our external publishing service, Postiz, carrying over the content and associated media.
4. Click **Create** to save the post

## AI Suggestions

Get AI-powered content suggestions:

1. Click **Get AI Suggestions** when creating/editing a post
2. The AI will generate:
   - Post variations
   - Hashtag recommendations
   - Optimal posting times
   - Engagement tips

3. Click **Use This** to apply a suggestion

### Configuring AI
- Go to **Settings** → **AI Configuration**
- Enter your Gemini API key
- Test the connection
- Adjust creativity level (temperature)

## Scheduling Posts

### Manual Scheduling
1. Set a **Scheduled Date** when creating a post
2. Change status to **Scheduled**
3. Post will be ready for publishing at the specified time

### Bulk Scheduling
1. Use the calendar view
2. Drag and drop posts to different dates
3. Adjust times as needed

## Filtering & Search

### Platform Filter
Click platform badges to filter by:
- All platforms
- Instagram only
- Facebook only
- LinkedIn only
- Twitter only

### Advanced Filters
Click **Advanced Filters** to filter by:
- **Status**: Draft, Ready for Review, Scheduled, Published
- **Date Range**: From/To dates
- **Promotion**: Link to specific promotional periods
- **Event**: Link to specific events

### Clearing Filters
Click **Clear All** to reset all filters

## View Modes

### Table View
- Compact list of all posts
- Quick editing
- Sortable columns
- Bulk actions

### Grid View
- Visual card layout
- Preview images
- Better for content review
- Mobile-friendly

## Linking Content

### Link to Promotions
1. Open a post
2. Select **Promotion Period**
3. Track posts by campaign

### Link to Events
1. Open a post
2. Select **Event**
3. Coordinate social media with events

## Offline Mode

Social media works offline:
- ✅ View all posts
- ✅ Create drafts (synced when online)
- ✅ Edit posts (synced when online)
- ⚠️ AI suggestions require internet connection

## Tips

- **Use AI wisely**: Get suggestions for inspiration, then personalize
- **Schedule ahead**: Plan content calendar weeks in advance
- **Link to campaigns**: Connect posts to promotions for better tracking
- **Filter efficiently**: Use advanced filters to find specific content
- **Draft first**: Create drafts, review, then schedule
- **Platform-specific**: Tailor content for each platform's audience
- **Track performance**: Use status updates to track what's published
