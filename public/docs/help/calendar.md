# Calendar

The Calendar provides a visual timeline of your marketing activities.

## Views
Switch between **Month**, **Week**, and **Day** views to see your schedule at different granularities.

## Events
The calendar displays:
- Project deadlines.
- Task due dates.
- Scheduled social media posts.
- Marketing events and promotions.

## Scheduling
Click on a date to quickly add a new event or reminder. Drag and drop items to reschedule them (if supported for that item type).
