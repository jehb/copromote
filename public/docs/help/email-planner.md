# Email Planner Help

## Overview
The Email Planner allows you to draft, manage, and schedule newsletter campaigns. It offers a structured way to plan your email content and link creative assets before they go out.

## Creating an Email Campaign

1. Click **Create Email** or **New Newsletter**.
2. Fill in the email details:
   - **Subject Line**: The subject of your email.
   - **Content**: The body copy for your newsletter.
   - **Scheduled Date**: When the email should be sent out.
   - **Status**: Manage stages such as Draft, Ready for Review, or Sent.
3. Click **Save** to create the draft.

## Linking Assets

You can attach images and creative files from your Gallery to your newsletter drafts:
1. Open the email in the Email Planner.
2. In the asset section, select **Attach Assets**.
3. Choose one or more images or files from your existing gallery or upload a new one.
4. The assets will be mapped to the newsletter for easy reference during final distribution.

## View Modes

- **Table View**: Browse all scheduled emails in a compact list layout.
- **Card View**: A richer visual layout, displaying the attached assets, events, and linked products side-by-side for a compact overview.

## Integration
Email drafts can be coordinated with promotional events and your overall marketing calendar to ensure consistent messaging across all channels.
