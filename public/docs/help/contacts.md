# Contacts Help

## Overview
The Contacts page helps you manage your professional network and relationships. Keep track of individuals, their organizations, and communication history.

## Adding a Contact

1. Click the **Add Contact** button in the top-right corner
2. Fill in the contact details:
   - **Name**: First and last name (required)
   - **Email**: Primary email address
   - **Phone**: Contact phone number
   - **Company**: Organization name
   - **Title**: Job title or role
   - **Notes**: Additional information

3. Click **Save** to create the contact

## Searching Contacts

Use the search bar to quickly find contacts:
- Search by **name**, **company**, or **email**
- Results update as you type
- Search is case-insensitive

## Adding Contacts to Organizations

You can link contacts to organizations in two ways:

### Method 1: From Contact Details
1. Open a contact
2. Click **Add to Organization**
3. Select an existing organization or create a new one

### Method 2: From Organizations Page
1. Go to the Organizations page
2. Open an organization
3. Click **Add Contact**
4. Select from your existing contacts

## Editing Contacts

1. Click on any contact in the list
2. Update the information
3. Click **Save Changes**

## Deleting Contacts

1. Click on a contact
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm the deletion

> **Note**: Deleting a contact will remove them from all associated organizations.

## Offline Mode

Contacts work fully offline:
- ✅ View all contacts
- ✅ Search contacts
- ✅ Create new contacts (synced when online)
- ✅ Edit contacts (synced when online)
- ✅ Delete contacts (synced when online)

Changes made offline will sync automatically when you reconnect.

## Tips

- **Keep it updated**: Regularly update contact information to maintain accuracy
- **Use notes**: Add context about your relationship or last interaction
- **Link to organizations**: Connect contacts to their companies for better organization
- **Search efficiently**: Use partial names or company names to find contacts quickly
